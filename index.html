Dashboard: Wildfires in the United States

Synopsis: A review of climate and geographical data about wildfires in the US. The data was pulled from an existing SQLLite database, manipulated in Pandas, and exported as a CSV, upon which we built the following visualizations in JavaScript. Key insights:

    1. Identify potential areas/climates that pose a higher risk for wildfires.
    2. Identify the leading drivers of wildfires (wildfire causes).
    3. Compare these drivers to wildfire size to better understand which wildfire causes create the most damage.
    4. Identify fire seasonality changes over time (heatmap).

Application: This information would be useful for the Real Estate/ Insurance industries who need to consider fire risk as part of their assessment for product offerings and for potential home/property owners who are evaluating the wildfire risks of future purchases.
